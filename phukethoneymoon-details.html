Thailand Honeymoon Getaways

Phuket Romance Escape

Celebrate your love in Phuket, where pristine beaches, luxury resorts, breathtaking sunsets, and unforgettable romantic experiences create the perfect honeymoon getaway.

5+

About This Tour

Phuket is one of Thailand's most romantic destinations, offering couples the perfect combination of luxury, relaxation, and adventure. From private beachfront villas to crystal-clear waters, every moment feels special.

Couples can enjoy island-hopping adventures, sunset cruises, spa treatments, and candlelight dinners overlooking the Andaman Sea. The island's stunning scenery creates unforgettable memories for newlyweds.

Whether you're relaxing by an infinity pool, exploring hidden beaches, or watching the sunset together, Phuket provides the perfect setting for a dream honeymoon.

Itinerary

  • Day 01: Arrival & Romantic Welcome

    Arrive at Phuket International Airport and enjoy a private transfer to your luxury beachfront resort. Relax with welcome drinks, unwind by the ocean, and end your first evening with a romantic candlelight dinner overlooking the Andaman Sea.

  • Day 02: Phuket Sightseeing Tour

    Explore Phuket's iconic attractions including Big Buddha, Wat Chalong Temple, Karon Viewpoint, and Phuket Old Town. Capture stunning photographs, discover local culture, and enjoy free time at Patong Beach during the evening.

  • Day 03: Phi Phi Island Adventure

    Embark on an exciting speedboat journey to the world-famous Phi Phi Islands. Visit Maya Bay, enjoy snorkeling in crystal-clear waters, relax on pristine beaches, and experience one of Thailand's most beautiful island destinations.

  • Day 04: Couple Spa & Sunset Cruise

    Enjoy a rejuvenating couple spa experience followed by leisure time at your resort. In the evening, board a romantic sunset cruise where you'll witness breathtaking views of the Andaman Sea while creating unforgettable memories together.

  • Day 05: Departure

    Enjoy breakfast at the resort before spending your final hours relaxing or shopping for souvenirs. Check out and transfer to Phuket International Airport with wonderful honeymoon memories to cherish forever.
