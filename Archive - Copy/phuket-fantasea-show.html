Phuket Fantasea Show

Phuket Fantasea Show

About Phuket Fantasea Show

Phuket FantaSea is a world-famous cultural theme park that combines Thai traditions, spectacular stage performances, acrobatics, special effects, and grand theatrical productions. Visitors can also enjoy a buffet dinner, shopping village, and family-friendly entertainment.


Attractions

Fantasy of a Kingdom Show: Experience Phuket FantaSea's signature theatrical performance featuring Thai mythology, cultural storytelling, dazzling costumes, acrobatics, and spectacular special effects.

Festival Village: Explore a vibrant cultural village filled with traditional Thai architecture, souvenir shops, carnival-style games, and handcrafted local products.

Golden Kinnaree Buffet Restaurant: Enjoy an extensive buffet featuring Thai and international cuisine in one of Asia's largest themed dining venues.

Cultural Performances: Discover traditional Thai dance, music, and artistic performances that showcase the country's rich heritage and traditions.

Tiger Jungle Adventure: Walk through beautifully designed themed areas inspired by Thai legends and mythical creatures while enjoying interactive displays and attractions.

Shopping & Entertainment: Browse unique gift shops, enjoy family-friendly entertainment, and capture memorable photos throughout the park.


Highlights

  • World-famous cultural theme park in Phuket
  • Spectacular "Fantasy of a Kingdom" stage show
  • Stunning costumes, acrobatics, and special effects
  • Traditional Thai culture and mythology
  • Golden Kinnaree international buffet restaurant
  • Festival Village shopping and entertainment area
  • Perfect for families, couples, and groups

Show Timings

Park Opens 5:30 PM
Buffet Dinner 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM
Theater Gates Open 8:40 PM
Main Show Starts 9:00 PM
Main Show Ends 10:10 PM
Park Closes 11:30 PM

Guests are encouraged to arrive early to explore the Festival Village, enjoy shopping and entertainment, and have dinner before the main show begins.

Ticket Information

  • Standard Show Ticket: From $50 per person
  • Show + Buffet Dinner: From $60 per person
  • Gold Seat + Buffet: From $75 per person
  • Children: Special rates may apply

Ticket prices may vary depending on the package selected, travel season, and availability.


Visitor Tips

  • Arrive at least 1 hour before the show to explore Festival Village and enjoy the attractions.
  • Book your tickets in advance, especially during peak tourist seasons.
  • Photography and video recording are not permitted inside the theater.
  • Wear comfortable clothing and footwear as you may spend several hours walking around the park.
  • Enjoy the buffet dinner before the show starts to make the most of your visit.
  • Keep your ticket and identification handy for a smooth entry process.
  • Check the weather forecast if you plan to explore the outdoor areas of the park.
  • Arrive early to secure the best seats if your package does not include reserved seating.
  • Families with children should plan extra time to enjoy the games, shopping, and entertainment zones.
  • Respect the park rules and follow staff instructions throughout your visit.
